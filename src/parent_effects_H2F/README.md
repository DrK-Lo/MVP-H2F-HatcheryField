# Parent effects

Folder for code related to modeling the effects of parental genetics and environment-of-origin on oyster performance (survival, length, condition index) across hatchery, nursery, and field stages.

| File | Function | Datasets used | Datasets produced |
|------|----------|---------------|-------------------|
| Q2_envr_quantile_models_field.Rmd | Models drivers of field survival and length using parental genetic diversity (Ho, allelic richness) and environment-of-origin quantiles (salinity Q10/Q90, temperature Q10) as predictors, separately by site and time point. Drops temp Q90 upfront due to collinearity. Uses backward stepwise selection. Produces scaled coefficient slope plots across time points. | `field_surv_all_times.csv` (from Q1_field_performance_models.Rmd), `field_length_all_times.csv` (from Q1_field_performance_models.Rmd), `parent_genetic_effects.csv` (from Fstats_experimental.Rmd), `envr_quantiles_allsites.csv` | **Figures (PDF):** `Fig3A_lew_surv_quantiles.pdf`, `Fig3C_york_surv_quantiles.pdf`, `FigS5A_lew_length_quantiles.pdf`, `FigS5C_york_length_quantiles.pdf`, `FigS2_corrplot_envr_quantiles_H0_Ar.pdf` | **Results (TXT):** `survival_model1_quantiles_results_<date>.txt`, `length_model1_quantiles_results_<date>.txt` |
| Q2_envrdist_models_field.Rmd | Models drivers of field survival and length using parental genetic diversity (Ho, allelic richness) and scaled Euclidean environmental distance (EoO to each common garden site) as predictors, separately by site and time point. Computes residuals of survival/length ~ Ho + Ar for downstream residual plots. | `field_surv_all_times.csv` (from Q1_field_performance_models.Rmd), `field_length_all_times.csv` (from Q1_field_performance_models.Rmd), `parent_genetic_effects.csv` (from Fstats_experimental.Rmd), `envr_quantiles_dist_all.csv` | **Figures (PDF):** `Fig3B_lew_surv_envrdist.pdf`, `Fig3D_york_surv_envrdist.pdf`, `FigS5B_lew_len_envrdist.pdf`, `FigS5D_york_len_envrdist.pdf`, `FigS8A_corrplot_lew_envrdist_H0_Ar.pdf`, `FigS8B_corrplot_york_envrdist_H0_Ar.pdf` | **Results (TXT):** `survival_model2_envrdist_results_<date>.txt`, `length_model2_envrdist_results_<date>.txt` | **Data (CSV):** `residuals_distance_vs_surv.csv`, `residuals_distance_vs_length.csv` |
| Q2_condition_index_models_field.Rmd | Models drivers of field condition index using two approaches: (1) Ho/Ar + environmental quantiles, and (2) Ho/Ar + environmental distance. Uses LMMs with bag as random effect, backward stepwise selection, and scaled slope plots. | `MVP_phenotyping_final.csv`, `parent_genetic_effects.csv` (from Fstats_experimental.Rmd), `envr_quantiles_allsites.csv`, `envr_quantiles_dist_all.csv` | **Figures (PDF):** `FigS7A_lew_ci_quantiles.pdf`, `FigS7B_lew_ci_envrdist.pdf`, `FigS7C_york_ci_quantiles.pdf`, `FigS7D_york_ci_envrdist.pdf` | **Results (TXT):** `condition_model1_quantiles_results_<date>.txt`, `condition_model2_envrdist_results_<date>.txt` |
| Q2_models_hatchery_nursery.Rmd | Models effects of parental genetic diversity and environmental quantiles on larval length (day 21 and day 77) and larval survival (day 21) in the hatchery/nursery using multiple regression. Produces scaled coefficient slope plot comparing hatchery and nursery stages. | `nursery_length.csv` (from Q1_nursery_performance.Rmd), `envr_quantiles_allsites.csv`, `CViMVP_larvae_survival.csv`, `parent_genetic_effects.csv` (from Fstats_experimental.Rmd) | **Figures (PDF):** `FigS6_hatchery_nursery_surv_quantiles.pdf` | **Results (TXT):** `hatchery_nursery_model_results_<date>.txt` |
| envrdist_residuals_plots.Rmd | Plots survival and length residuals (after removing Ho + Ar effects) against environmental distance to each common garden site, colored by source population. Tests whether southern populations drive the positive relationship between environmental distance and performance. | `field_surv_all_times.csv`, `envr_quantiles_dist_all.csv`, `residuals_distance_vs_surv.csv` (from Q2_envrdist_models_field.Rmd), `residuals_distance_vs_length.csv` (from Q2_envrdist_models_field.Rmd) | **Figures (PDF):** `FigS9A_lew_surv_envrdist_residuals_t3.pdf`, `FigS9B_lew_length_envrdist_residuals_t3.pdf`, `FigS9C_york_surv_envrdist_residuals_t3.pdf`, `FigS9D_york_length_envrdist_residuals_t3.pdf` |
